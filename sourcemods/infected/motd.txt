<h1><i>INFECTED</i></h1>

You are playing Infected, a HAlf-life 2:DeathMatch Mod.  Infected is a Humans versus Zombies DeathMatch.  As a Human, stay alive as long as possible by slowing down the Zombies coming after you.  If you are killed, you have become Infected meaning you are now a Zombie.  Try to be the last Zombie alive by keeping all the Zombies away from you.

To view the inspiration to this game, please <a href="http://youtu.be/e2uD0z8A1eQ">click-here.</a>
